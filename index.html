Proof Of Image
You will find the fundamentals of the blockchain and crypto world, explained in an ironic way.
All the informations comes with theoretical slides overlaided by images, insights, illustrations, links, and QR codes for further informations.
I also reported some of my thoughts about the blockchain and its world: every day something changes, novelties come out daily and can turn all your ideas upside down.

Proof Of Concept

Proof of image aims to introduce anyone with any background to the blockchain world.
This book features the basics of the crypto and blockchain world explained in a simple and concise way understandable to anyone.
It must be kept in mind that it is a world that bases its foundation on open source, consequently in-depth and detailed explanations of all the topics covered in the book can be found on the web.
